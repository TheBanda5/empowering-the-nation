About Us

Founded in 2018, Empowering the Nation was created with a singular goal: to transform the lives of domestic workers and gardeners by equipping them with essential skills for better job opportunities and professional growth. Our mission is to provide comprehensive training programs that focus on practical, real-world applications that enable our participants to not only improve their employability but also gain confidence in their abilities.

At Empowering the Nation, we believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to achieve success through education and skills development. Our range of courses, from First Aid to Life Skills, helps participants acquire both hard and soft skills that are in demand in the modern job market.

We are proud to offer a variety of programs, including six-month and six-week courses, designed to fit into the busy lives of our students.
